This page is dedicated to legendary guitarists who personally inspired me

I would like to share a part of my passion in music, and share information on some of my favorite guitarists. Following musicians have deeply influenced me and my approach to education in music. They are know to be some of the most influential and significant guitarists in the history of guitar as a musical instrument.

John Petrucci

John Petrucci is a guitarist in one of my most favorite music bands - Dream Theater. He is fast, he is strong , he is metal.

Steve Vai

Steve Vai is truly one of the coolest and most influential guitarists. He is very individualistic - there is really no person on the planet to express feelings in a guitar as he does.
